This page will talk about 5 solutions to the problem car pollution.

The pollution from vehicles is a major cause of health problems such as asthma. Everyone benefits from clean air. If you follow these actions, then the amount of car pollution will decrease.

1. Ride a Bike or Walk

If you're only going a short distance, ride a bike or walk instead of driving. You can get exercise and enjoy the fresh air.

2. Take Public Transit

If you need to go somewhere that is along a bus or light rail line, consider taking public transit instead of going in a car.

3. Carpool

When going to school or work, go with other people who are going to the same place. You can save money and reduce the amount of fuel used at the same time.

4. Avoid Idling

When idling, you waste fuel when you don't move. If you'll be in the same spot for a while, turn off your veichle's engine (as long as it is safe to do so).

5. Use Alternative Fuels

Alter native fuels are cleaner than regular gasoline or diesel. Alternative fuel vehicles include electric vehicles and flex-fuel vehicles that can use ethanol blends.
